What is Mosquito Mesh?

Mosquito mesh is a protective screen made from fine, high-quality materials such as stainless steel, fiberglass, or aluminum. It is designed to prevent mosquitoes, flies, and other insects from entering your home while allowing fresh air and natural light to pass through.
