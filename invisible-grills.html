Enhance Safety with Stylish & Secure Invisible Grills

Are you looking for a safe, durable, and aesthetically pleasing alternative to traditional metal grills? Invisible grills are the perfect modern solution for balconies, windows, staircases, and open spaces, providing unmatched security without obstructing your view.

At V-Safe, we offer high-quality invisible grills designed for residential and commercial spaces, ensuring maximum protection, durability, and style.

What Are Invisible Grills?

Invisible grills are high-strength stainless steel cables coated with nylon or PVC, installed in a grid-like pattern on balconies, windows, or staircases. These grills provide the same level of security as traditional metal grills while maintaining an open and unobstructed view.

Our V-Safe Invisible Grills are made from marine-grade 316 stainless steel, ensuring they are rustproof, weather-resistant, and capable of withstanding heavy impact.

Benefits of Invisible Grills

  • Unobstructed View & Elegant Aesthetics:
    • Unlike traditional metal grills, invisible grills offer a clear and open view, making them perfect for high-rise apartments.
    • Their sleek and modern design enhances the beauty of your home.
  • Maximum Safety & High Durability:
    • Made from 316-grade stainless steel cables that can withstand up to 400 kg of force.
    • Ensures protection against falls, break-ins, and accidents.
  • Rustproof & Weather-Resistant:
    • Unlike iron grills that rust over time, invisible grills are completely corrosion-resistant, ensuring long-lasting durability.
    • Ideal for coastal areas, high-rise buildings, and extreme weather conditions.
  • Child & Pet-Friendly Design:
    • Provides a secure barrier for children and pets, preventing falls from balconies or windows.
    • Safe for pets as it prevents them from slipping through gaps.
  • Strong Yet Invisible Protection:
    • The grills are only 2-3mm thick, making them almost invisible from a distance.
    • Despite their sleek design, they are strong enough to provide excellent security.
  • Minimal Maintenance & Easy Cleaning:
    • Unlike traditional grills that require frequent repainting and rust treatment, invisible grills require almost no maintenance.
    • Easy to clean with just a damp cloth.
  • Customizable for Any Space:
    • Available in custom sizes and designs to fit balconies, windows, staircases, terraces, and more.
    • Can be installed horizontally or vertically based on preference.
