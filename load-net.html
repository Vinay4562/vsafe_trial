What is a Load Net?

A Load Net (also known as a Cargo Net) is a strong, flexible mesh used to secure goods, prevent movement during transport, and provide protection in storage areas. These nets are made from heavy-duty nylon, polypropylene, or polyester and come with reinforced edges and hooks for extra strength.

With V-Safe Load Nets, you can transport and store goods safely and efficiently.
