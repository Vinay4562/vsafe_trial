Safety Nets

Home safety nets are protective barriers installed on balconies, windows, and staircases to prevent accidental falls. Made from high-quality, durable nylon or polyethylene, they are weather-resistant and long-lasting. These nets are child and pet-friendly, ensuring safety without compromising ventilation or visibility. They are customizable in size and design to fit different home structures. Easy to install and maintain, home safety nets provide a secure and cost-effective safety solution for modern homes.

Invisible Grilles

Invisible grilles are modern safety solutions for windows, balconies, and staircases, offering security without blocking the view. Made of high-tensile stainless steel, they are rust-resistant, durable, and withstand up to 400 kg of force. Customizable in design, they come in fixed, sliding, or casement styles for various needs. Child and pet-friendly, they ensure safety while allowing proper ventilation. With minimal maintenance and optional alarm systems, they are a sleek, secure alternative to traditional metal bars.
